Essential Spring Cleaning Tips for a Fresh Start

Published: June 25, 2023 By King of Clean DC Team

Why Spring Cleaning Matters

Room-by-Room Spring Cleaning Guide

Kitchen

  • Deep clean appliances (refrigerator, oven, microwave)
  • Declutter and organize pantry
  • Clean cabinet fronts and handles
  • Descale coffee maker and kettle
  • Clean and disinfect garbage disposal

Living Room

  • Deep clean upholstery and curtains
  • Dust and clean light fixtures
  • Clean windows and window tracks
  • Vacuum under furniture
  • Clean and organize entertainment center

Bedrooms

  • Rotate and flip mattresses
  • Wash all bedding, including duvets
  • Clean under bed storage
  • Organize closets and drawers
  • Clean ceiling fans and light fixtures

Bathrooms

  • Deep clean grout lines
  • Descale shower heads and faucets
  • Clean exhaust fans
  • Organize medicine cabinets
  • Replace shower curtain liners

Spring Cleaning Checklist

Follow this order for maximum efficiency:

  1. Declutter and organize
  2. Dust from top to bottom
  3. Clean windows and window treatments
  4. Deep clean floors and carpets
  5. Focus on forgotten areas

Professional Tips

  • Work from top to bottom in each room
  • Use microfiber cloths to prevent scratches
  • Don't forget often-overlooked areas like doorknobs and light switches
  • Clean your cleaning tools before starting
  • Open windows for ventilation while cleaning
