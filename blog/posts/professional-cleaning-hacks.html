After decades of combined experience, our professional cleaning team has developed numerous tricks and techniques that make cleaning more efficient and effective. Today, we're sharing our best-kept secrets!

Kitchen Cleaning Secrets

1. Grease-Busting Tricks

  • Use dish soap before degreaser on tough stains
  • Apply baking soda paste for baked-on grease
  • Steam clean microwave with lemon water
  • Use vinegar-water solution for daily maintenance

2. Appliance Cleaning Hacks

  • Clean refrigerator coils with specialized brush
  • Use dishwasher tablet to clean washing machine
  • Remove mineral deposits with citric acid
  • Clean oven with steam before scrubbing

Bathroom Professional Secrets

1. Shower and Tub Magic

  • Apply car wax to prevent soap scum
  • Use dryer sheets to clean soap scum
  • Prevent mold with daily ventilation routine
  • Clean grout with oxygen bleach paste

2. Toilet Cleaning Tricks

  • Use coca-cola for tough stains
  • Apply vinegar under rim weekly
  • Clean tank interior monthly
  • Use pumice stone for mineral rings
